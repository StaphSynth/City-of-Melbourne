Situated on the Yarra River, Melbourne is a major centre of trade, industry and culture in Australia. The multicultural tapestry of the city affords a unique mix of attractions, entertainment and dining to suit any taste.

  • The National Gallery of Victoria is one of the world's most visited art museums.

  • Albert Park, one of several inner-urban parks, which also hosts the Melbourne Grand Prix.

  • The Melbourne Cricket Ground, centre of the city's sporting culture.
